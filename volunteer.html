Construction volunteers join projects in the Hambanthota area to work on public buildings and schools, typically in communities in need of assistance and funding. Construction & Renovation Support
  • ✅Duration: 1 - 12 weeks
  • ✅Program Fees from $400
  • ✅Programs start every Monday
  • ✅Temples, monasteries and schools
  • ✅Volunteers aged 16+ and families

Ruhuna construction projects are focused on home building, school, and orphanage renovation, and building playground and bathroom facilities for areas that do not have them. Volunteers work with our foreman, who provides guidance and training, along with families and other volunteers. Volunteers should come ready to work and to work hard, as they'll be laying brick, mixing concrete, doing carpentry, painting, laying tile, hauling materials, and landscaping/gardening. Depending on the phase of the project they participate in, they may also be asked to help prepare or clean up the construction site. Volunteers are not required to have experience with construction work.

Construction of School Library at Government School
  • ✅Duration: 1 - 12 weeks
  • ✅Program Fees from $500
  • ✅Programs start every Monday
  • ✅Temples, monasteries and schools
  • ✅Volunteers aged 16+ and families

We started building up a primary school from scratch because there was none around in the radius of 7 kilometers. This means the children had to walk for 14 kilometers back and forth under the scorching heat without shoes. It was too stressful and discouraging to acquire education in this area. We are looking for volunteers to help us FINISH this project with a bang!!! We still have a library, and arts theatre to build and Health care centre to complete. Join us in doing a work out with a purpose through construction, landscaping and environmental conservation activities. We are open to sustainable ideas and initiatives. Let us know how you can help us realize this dream by joining us.
